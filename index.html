About algorix

Teaching computer science can be tedious for even experienced instructors. Searching for an effective LMS capable of instructing students based on ability can become especially exasperating.

Algorix resolves this issue. It provides an environment in which educators can create customizable classrooms and problem sets to facilitate coding education for students. Through Algorix's submission and feedback process, every student experience is constantly tracked and accessible through the dashboard. 

All coding classes, from AP computer science to even undergraduate courses, benefit from the improved instructional environment of Algorix. For more information, please contact us through email below.
